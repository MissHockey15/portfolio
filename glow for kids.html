Glow for Kids

Deliverables

Logo

Challenge

Design a fun and youth full logo for the Boys and Girls Club to use for their upcoming Glow for Kids event.

Outcome

To fit the glow in the dark theme I designed a neon light logo that can be interchanged with the different brand colours.

Glow for Kids is an event run by the Boys and Gurls Club in Ottawa. It is Ottawa's only glow in the dark dodgeball tournament that engages many different people from alumni to teams to young professionals. They reached out to me to help design a logo to use for the event.
